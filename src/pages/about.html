🎭 The Origin Story

📅 Once upon a time, this was supposed to be a simple 1-day joke...

Hello! I'm weeniemount, the creator and reluctant maintainer of this chaotic image hosting service. What you're experiencing right now was never supposed to exist for more than 24 hours.

I thought it would be funny to make a parody of image hosts that probably spy on their users. You know the type - those sketchy sites that definitely do weird stuff with your data. So I made this deliberately chaotic alternative with a ridiculous name as a joke.

Plot twist: I accidentally made one that's similar-ish to the others I tried to parody. Whoops! Turns out making a functional image host is harder than making fun of them. Who knew? 🤷‍♂️

💡 Plot Twist: People actually started using it. Like, a LOT of people. Suddenly I had users, traffic, and responsibilities. What have I done?

🌐 The Domain Situation

🎊 We actually got a proper domain! pissandshitimages.com is now a real website that exists on the internet. My mom would be so proud (please don't tell her).

Getting the domain was both the best and worst decision ever. On one hand, it's legitimately hilarious that this URL exists. On the other hand, it means I'm probably stuck maintaining this thing forever now. The domain costs money, which means I'm financially invested in keeping "pissandshitimages.com" alive. What a sentence to write.

🎲 What Even Is This Thing?

For those just discovering this chaos, here's what you've stumbled into:

  • 🖼️ An image hosting service with intentional quality destruction
  • 🎰 Gambling mechanics that determine how badly your images get "shitified" (thanks to tovade's Copilot AI that was literally on crack!)
  • 🎭 A gallery full of gloriously destroyed memes and photos
  • 🤡 The product of one person's questionable sense of humor and some very chaotic AI assistance
  • 📊 Surprisingly robust infrastructure for what started as a joke (when it's not being broken by said AI)
💥 Fun fact: The RNG gambling system came from tovade's Copilot AI which was apparently having a very chaotic day and decided to add gambling mechanics AND break the site 4 times in a row. Sometimes the best features come from controlled chaos!
